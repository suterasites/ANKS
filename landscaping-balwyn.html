In Balwyn

An outdoor environment to match the build.

Balwyn is defined by its blocks and its rebuilds. The school zone has driven a steady wave of knock-down-rebuilds, and the suburb's generous, often north-facing blocks give a landscape real room to work. The brief here is usually a substantial contemporary family home that needs the outdoor environment to match, driveway, pool surrounds, level changes and planting resolved against the architecture.

We run that as one piece of work. Earthworks and retaining handle the level changes these blocks often carry, hardscape and structure are detailed against the home, and planting is chosen to establish well and give a new build the setting it was drawn for. One in-house team, from first excavation to final handover.

Common questions

Landscape construction in Balwyn.

Do you handle pools, retaining and level changes on Balwyn rebuild blocks?

Yes. Many Balwyn blocks carry a fall and a substantial new build, so retaining, drainage and level changes are usually part of the brief. We resolve the earthworks and structure against the architectural levels from day one, so pool surrounds, terraces and driveways sit correctly and drain properly rather than being reworked at the end.

Do you work across Balwyn?

Yes. Balwyn sits inside our core eastern and south-eastern service area. We deliver architectural landscape construction, commercial works and builder external-works packages across Balwyn and the surrounding suburbs.

Is it one team or subcontractors?

One in-house team. Landscape, construction and earthworks all sit under one company with a single line of accountability, so the work resolves as a single piece rather than three trades stitched together at the end.

Where can we see your work?

Our portfolio includes recent residential work in North Balwyn and Sunbury and commercial grounds at Mernda Hills and St James, Vermont. Every project shown is real ANKS work.
