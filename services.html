The approach

Landscape, construction and earthworks resolve together.

Most landscape projects fail at the joins. The earthworks team finishes and leaves. The landscape team arrives weeks later, finds the levels are wrong, the drainage is in the wrong place, the retaining doesn't sit where the planting needs it. The brief that resolved beautifully on paper gets stitched together on site.

ANKS operates differently. The three disciplines sit under one in-house team with a single line of accountability. Levels, drainage, structure and planting are resolved together from day one, because the same team is doing all of it.

Three disciplines

What sits under one team.

Landscape

Architectural landscape construction

Hardscape, paving, planting, lighting and finishing. Crazy paving, bluestone, corten, layered beds, feature trees and turf, detailed against the architecture of the site.

Construction

Structural & civil construction

Boulder, sandstone, masonry and concrete retaining. Pool surrounds, terraces, driveways, kerbs, services trenches and structural elements that anchor the landscape.

Earthworks

Excavation & site preparation

Bulk and detail excavation, cut and fill, sub-base preparation, drainage and levels coordinated against the architectural drawings before construction begins.
